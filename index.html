TAARANGANA

Taarangana, the annual cultural fest of IGDTUW, is not just a name, it is an emotion that brings us all together. The festival carves out a niche for itself because of the resolute women driving it.
After nine massively successful editions, the cultural extravaganza is all set to return in February with its 10th edition, Taarangana'23. A spectacle to behold, Taarangana witnesses a gathering of zealous students from various colleges across the city who meet, mingle, and share artistic and intellectual interests while making precious memories. Through a wide variety of events in music, dance, theatre, and other artistic domains, this two-day cultural blast offers innovative students an opportunity and platform to showcase their astounding talents, turning the campus into an ocean of culture, excitement, and opportunities. The main aim of Taarangana has always been to bring out the best talents and showcase them to the world, and we are more prepared than ever before.

G20, or Group of Twenty is an intergovernmental forum that brings together 19 countries and the European Union (EU) to address global economic issues like international financial stability, climate change mitigation, and sustainable development. The annual cultural extravaganza of IGDTUW, Taarangana'23, is joining hands with the G20 to celebrate one of the biggest cultural fests in Delhi. In Taarangana'23, we intend to incorporate the values and agenda of the G20, in order to help people understand and appreciate the organization's principles while also enjoying the cultural festival.
